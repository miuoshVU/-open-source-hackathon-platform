Rules and Regulations, Terms of Service

Event Description
The Christmas Challenge is an online hackathon organized by Parkyeri AS of Istanbul, Turkey. This event is designed to engage and empower developers worldwide by offering them an opportunity to contribute to open-source projects. Participant(s) may work individually or as a group to resolve issues and enhance the open-source community. Participant(s) may choose to utilize our platform or their own platform and/or preferred tools. This hackathon is a chance to make a meaningful impact.

Event Dates and Format
- The Christmas Challenge will be conducted entirely online, allowing participant(s) to join from via the Internet site https://challenge.parkyericom/ .
- The challenge starts December 1st and ends on December 25th, 2023. Participant(s) may participate at any time during this period.

Eligibility
- The Christmas Challenge is open to all developers, regardless of their background or experience.
- There are no age restrictions, and anyone with an interest in coding and open-source projects can participate. However, there are restrictions on winning a prize.
- We reserve the right to refuse anyone as a participant or winner, with or without a reason.

Registration
- Participation in the Christmas Challenge is completely free. Simply register on our platform to get started on the hackathon journey.

Team Formation
- Participant(s) are invited to exhibit their skills and creativity in a solo capacity. While the creation of groups is permitted, it is imperative to note that the submission of work shall be consolidated into a singular entry. This provision is in place to accommodate the potential involvement of software houses or companies seeking to contribute collectively. The consolidation of submissions ensures a streamlined and fair assessment process, facilitating the effective evaluation of each participant or group's contribution to the competition.
- Many people can work as a group; however, only one invidiual representing the team can submit their work, and only one physical person can be selected as a winner.

Development Containers
- Parkyeri may provide compute resources that participant(s) may use to work on their submission to win a prize, but is under no obligation to do so.
- To be eligible to use these resources, participant(s) must verify their e-mail addresses an phone numbers on the hackathon web page at https://challenge.parkyeri.com/
- To be eligible to use these resources, participant(s) must be eligible to win a prize.
- To be eligible to use these resources, participant(s) must have verified their e-mail addresses and phone numbers.

Prizes and Awards
- Not all participant(s) are eligible for a prize: Only physical persons who have the legal capacity to enter legal contracts on their own (typically older than 18 years and of sufficient mental strength), residing in and having a nationality from a country legally recognized by and with no sanctions applied by the Republic of Turkiye. Current employees and interns of Parkyeri are not eligible for prize(s).
- To be eligible for a prize, participant(s) must have submitted their application for a prize via the hackathon web site before the end of the Hackathon, December 25th, 2023, AoE timezone.
- The winner(s) of the Christmas Challenge will be rewarded with computing resources, listed below, which can be used during the calendar year of 2024.
- The cash value of the prize(s) is 0 (zero) TRY, Turkish Liras. Prizes cannot be converted to cash. The dates of the services provided as a prize can not be changed.

- The first prize will be awarded to only one winner. The prize is a cloud virtual server with 16 vcpu, 64GB RAM, 500GB hard disk, hosted at a datacenter facility chosen by Parkyeri.
- The second prize will be awared up to 4 winners. The prize is a cloud virtual development environment with 4 vcpu, 16GB RAM, 100GB hard disk, hosted at a datacenter facility chosen by Parkyeri.
- The third prize will be awared up to 16 winners. The prize is a cloud virtual development environment with 2 vcpu, 8GB RAM, 50GB hard disk, hosted at a datacenter facility chosen by Parkyeri.
- A certificate of achievement will be presented to the winner(s), upon demand.
- Only winners selected by the Jury may receive a prize.
- If a participant is selected to be a winner, but not eligible for a prize, the prize will not be awarded to another recipient.
