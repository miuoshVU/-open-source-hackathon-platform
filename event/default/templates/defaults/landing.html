How does it work?

  • Register to Particapte

    Developers kick off their journey by registering on our website, unlocking access to a development experience until Christmas.

  • Verify Your Email and Phone Number

    Participants verify their email and number within the dashboard, ensuring a safe and trusted environment

  • Claim Your Free Development Coding Space

    After verification, you can claim your free development container, which you can use to develop your contribution to open source.

  • Work On Your Open Source Contribution

    With verification complete, you can dive into the heart of working on your contributing to any open source project of your choice that aligns with your skills and passions.

  • Submit Your Work

    You can use the submission area in the dashboard, to submit your work to win a prize. It's the platform to present your hard work and emphasize and highlight the potential impact of your work.

  • Judging Process

    Expert judges carefully assess each submission, evaluating the impact and significance of the contributions to notable open source repositories.

  • Winner Announcement

    After rigorous evaluation, the winners emerge, recognized for their outstanding contributions. Prizes, including a one-year virtual machine access and certificates of achievement, await the champions.

  • Enjoy your servers for a year

    If you are selected to be a winner, you can use your compute resources for a full year.
